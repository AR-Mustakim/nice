ESPRE workshop was held at RE'18 in Banff. Yijun Yu and Lionel Briand gave very interesting keynote speeches on adaptive security and privacy requirements, and modeling security and privacy requirements to enable test automation, respectively. Thanks to all of you co-organizers, PC members and participants who made it happen!
